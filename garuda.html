Garuda Airlines Review Analysis

Sentiment analysis of customer reviews to identify service improvement opportunities and uncover key drivers of customer satisfaction patterns

Tableau NLP Sentiment Analysis Customer Insights

Project Overview

Garuda Indonesia, the flag carrier of Indonesia, needed insights into customer satisfaction patterns to improve their service quality and competitive positioning. Using natural language processing and sentiment analysis, I analyzed thousands of customer reviews to identify key satisfaction drivers and improvement opportunities.

This comprehensive analysis revealed critical insights about service quality, route performance, and customer experience factors that directly impact airline reputation and customer loyalty.

Key Discoveries

  • Food quality and variety were the strongest positive sentiment drivers
  • Flight delays and cancellations generated 65% of negative sentiment
  • Cabin crew service quality showed strong correlation with overall satisfaction
  • Route-specific patterns revealed significant service inconsistencies
  • Business class satisfaction was 40% higher than economy class

The Challenge

Garuda Indonesia was experiencing declining customer satisfaction scores but lacked detailed insights into specific service areas needing improvement. Traditional surveys provided limited feedback, while online reviews contained rich, unstructured data that was difficult to analyze at scale.

My Approach

I developed a comprehensive sentiment analysis pipeline that processed thousands of customer reviews from multiple platforms, categorized feedback by service area, and identified actionable improvement opportunities through statistical analysis and visualization.

Methodology

Data Collection

Gathered 5,000+ reviews from airline review platforms and social media

Text Processing

Applied NLP techniques for cleaning, tokenization, and feature extraction

Sentiment Classification

Visualization

Impact & Insights

The analysis provided Garuda Indonesia with a roadmap for service improvements based on actual customer feedback. Key recommendations included:

  • Operational Excellence: Focus on reducing flight delays through better scheduling and ground operations
  • Service Training: Implement targeted cabin crew training programs for consistency
  • Food & Beverage: Leverage positive food feedback as a competitive advantage
  • Route Optimization: Address service quality variations across different routes
  • Communication: Improve passenger communication during disruptions

Technical Achievements

This project demonstrated advanced NLP capabilities and business intelligence skills, including automated text processing, sentiment classification with 85% accuracy, and creation of actionable business insights from unstructured data.
